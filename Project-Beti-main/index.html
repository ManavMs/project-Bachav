What we do

For the under-privileged

We provide free pads to the under-privileged as many can't afford and still use cloth.

For the schools

We believe periods should not be a barrier and prevent girls from going schools, hence we also supply to schools those who need our help and contact us.

Stations

We will also set-up health stations as well as sub-stations with female employees. Pads will be distributed every month like an essential ration where people needto issue per their card.

Funds and Collaborations

We are ready to collaborate with NGOs and Government in terms of funds as well as reaching and helping more women. We also accept donations for our cause, more funds we have, more poor families will get our help.

Our Work So Far

We are happy to announce we have collaborated with schools and established few stations at the outskirts of Punjab and Chandigarh. We have a small production unit for low cost sanitary pads and we have also tied up with the government to support our cause with funds and equipments. We are open to work and collaborate with more NGOs and well as other organizations to help us reach more schools and under priviledged girls.

Schools

We are currently working with five schools in rural areas and providing them free sanitary pads for girls.

Stations

We have established 10 stations and 2 sub stations in Punjab and Chandigarh respectively. Each station have two women employees willing to work for the cause once a month.

Production

We have 20 people who are currently making low cost sanitary pads. The motive being women use less or no cloth instead using our pads for free and prevent infections and diseases.

Funds

We have raised around 1 lakh through donations and funds for our cause. As this is a big movement and we are trying to bring forth a change in peoples lives especially rural girls, we need more funds to help more people.
